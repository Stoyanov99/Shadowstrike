Detailed Impact & Findings

#01

Wildcard CORS policy (Access-Control-Allow-Origin: *)

HIGH
Detection Module: HEADERS
VULNERABILITY DETAILS:

Any website can make cross-origin requests to your domain. This is dangerous if any authenticated endpoints exist.

💸 BUSINESS RISK / IMPACT

Malicious websites can read sensitive user data (like account details or API keys) directly from the victim's browser session across origins.

🛠️ STEP-BY-STEP REMEDIATION

Restrict CORS to specific trusted origins.

#02

Missing header: X-Frame-Options

MEDIUM
Detection Module: HEADERS
VULNERABILITY DETAILS:

Missing X-Frame-Options header. Site can be embedded in iframes, enabling clickjacking attacks.

💸 BUSINESS RISK / IMPACT

Attackers can trick your visitors into clicking hidden buttons (Clickjacking), potentially leading to unauthorized actions like deleting accounts or transferring funds.

🛠️ STEP-BY-STEP REMEDIATION

Add header: X-Frame-Options: DENY

#03

Missing header: X-Content-Type-Options

MEDIUM
Detection Module: HEADERS
VULNERABILITY DETAILS:

Missing X-Content-Type-Options header. Browser may MIME-sniff content, leading to XSS.

💸 BUSINESS RISK / IMPACT

Malicious user uploads could be interpreted as executable scripts by the browser, leading to full Cross-Site Scripting (XSS) compromises.

🛠️ STEP-BY-STEP REMEDIATION

Add header: X-Content-Type-Options: nosniff

#04

Missing header: Content-Security-Policy

MEDIUM
Detection Module: HEADERS
VULNERABILITY DETAILS:

Missing Content-Security-Policy header. No restrictions on script/resource sources.

💸 BUSINESS RISK / IMPACT

Attackers can inject malicious scripts (XSS) into the browsers of your users, stealing their session cookies, credit cards, or defacing the website.

🛠️ STEP-BY-STEP REMEDIATION

Add a strict CSP header restricting script-src and other directives.

#05

Missing header: Referrer-Policy

LOW
Detection Module: HEADERS
VULNERABILITY DETAILS:

Missing Referrer-Policy header. Full URL may leak to third parties.

💸 BUSINESS RISK / IMPACT

Sensitive tokens or internal tracking IDs in URLs might leak to third-party analytics providers or external links.

🛠️ STEP-BY-STEP REMEDIATION

Add header: Referrer-Policy: strict-origin-when-cross-origin

#06

Missing header: Permissions-Policy

LOW
Detection Module: HEADERS
VULNERABILITY DETAILS:

Missing Permissions-Policy header. No restrictions on browser features (camera, mic).

💸 BUSINESS RISK / IMPACT

If compromised, the site might silently request access to the user's camera, microphone, or location without explicit centralized restriction.

🛠️ STEP-BY-STEP REMEDIATION

Add header: Permissions-Policy: camera=(), microphone=(), geolocation=()

#07

Server version disclosed — helps attackers fingerprint the technology. Value: Vercel

🛠️ STEP-BY-STEP REMEDIATION
